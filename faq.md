# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. Where is my financial data stored?
Fin OS Pro is an offline-first application. By default, all of your accounts, transactions, and budgets are encrypted and stored locally on your device's internal storage. We do not have servers, and we cannot see your financial data.

### 2. How does the Google Drive Sync work?
If you enable "Auto-Cloud Backup" in the Settings, the app will securely upload an encrypted copy of your data to your personal Google Drive. 

**Privacy Note:** The app uses a secure, hidden folder called `AppData`. This means Fin OS Pro can only access its own backup file. It cannot see, read, or access your personal photos, documents, or emails.

### 3. Can I import my old bank statements?
Yes! Fin OS Pro includes a **2026 CSV Migration Engine**. Go to the Data Management screen, download our official CSV template, map your bank's data to it, and import years of history in seconds.

### 4. What happens if I lose my phone?
If you did not enable Google Drive Sync, your data is lost with the device (as it is stored locally for your privacy). We highly recommend enabling Auto-Cloud Backup in the settings so you can easily restore your vault on a new device.

### 5. Why do some of my debts show an EMI and others show an Interest Rate?
Our Asset Manager automatically detects the type of liability. For structured loans (like Car Loans or Gold Loans), it tracks your fixed EMI. For revolving debt (like Credit Cards), it highlights the high interest rate to help you prioritize using the Debt Crusher simulator.

### 6. I'm locked out by the Biometric App Lock!
The Biometric lock relies on your device's native security (FaceID/Fingerprint). If your biometric scanner fails, your device will automatically prompt you for your phone's PIN or pattern as a fallback. 

### 7. How do I delete all my data?
Go to **Settings > Danger Zone** and tap **Reset App Data**. This will instantly wipe the local database clean. If you want to delete your cloud backup, you can remove Fin OS Pro's access directly from your Google Account Security dashboard.
